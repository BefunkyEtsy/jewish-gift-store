✡️ Jewish Gift-Giving Guide: 16 Life Events Worth Celebrating with a Thoughtful Present

Intro:
Jewish life is full of meaningful moments—each one a chance to show love, support, and blessings through a thoughtful gift. Whether you're celebrating a new baby, a Bar Mitzvah, or a Torah milestone, here's your go-to guide for the most common (and often overlooked) Jewish gifting occasions. We've included both meaningful Judaica and practical general gifts so you can find something for every personality and budget.
