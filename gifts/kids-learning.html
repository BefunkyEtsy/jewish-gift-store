📖 Kids' First Siddur / Chumash / School Gifts

Beginning formal Jewish education is a treasured moment in a child’s life. Whether it’s receiving their first siddur, starting day school, or learning the Aleph-Bet, these milestones deserve celebration with gifts that nurture pride, learning, and joy.

🕍 First Siddur & Chumash

  • Children’s Siddurim – Illustrated and age-appropriate prayer books.
  • Children’s Chumash – Torah texts for early learners.
  • Hebrew Prayer Books – Help them grow comfortable with daily tefillah.

🎁 Personalized Judaica & Keepsakes

  • Siddur or Chumash Covers – Embroidered with the child’s name.
  • Name Plaques – For bedroom doors or bookshelves.
  • Name Jewelry – Bracelets or necklaces with Hebrew letters.

📚 Learning Aids & Toys

  • Aleph-Bet Toys – Blocks, puzzles, and games to learn the Hebrew alphabet.
  • Jewish Storybooks – Fun and meaningful reads for young minds.
  • Tzedakah Boxes – Encourage giving from a young age.

🎒 Back-to-School Essentials

  • Backpacks with Jewish Themes – Fun, functional, and full of spirit.
  • Lunchboxes – Ideal for day school or cheder.
  • School Supplies – Pens, notebooks, and more with Jewish motifs.
