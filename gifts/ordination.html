🏛 Ordination & Rabbinic Titles: Gifts for New Rabbis and Torah Scholars

Receiving semicha (rabbinic ordination) is a momentous occasion, marking years of Torah learning and the beginning of a sacred role. These gifts honor that achievement with lasting value, utility, and respect.

📚 Torah Study Essentials

  • Talmud Sets – A foundation of advanced Torah learning.
  • Torah Book Sets – Full Chumash with commentary.
  • Mishnah Sets – Organized study of the Oral Torah.
  • Halacha Sets – Essential Jewish legal texts.
  • Jewish Law Books – Covering Shabbat, kashrut, and lifecycle topics.

🎁 Office & Personal Judaica

  • Rabbinic Plaques or Certificates – Commemorate their title with dignity.
  • Leather Briefcases – For transporting seforim and shiur notes.
  • Jewish Desk Accessories – Add reverence and function to their new office.

💻 Tech for Torah

  • iPad or Tablet – Perfect for learning on Sefaria or Otzar HaHochma.
  • Laptop – Great for writing divrei Torah, teshuvot, or preparing shiurim.
  • Digital Pens & Apps – Tools for tech-savvy Torah learning.
